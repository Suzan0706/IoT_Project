Metadata principles / Built on FAIR from the first byte.

01 / 04

Findable

Persistent identifiers, rich metadata, and indexed discovery layers ensure every dataset can be found by people and machines.

02 / 04

Accessible

Standardised retrieval protocols, licences and provenance trails guarantee long-term access to both data and its metadata.

03 / 04

Interoperable

Dublin Core–aligned schemas, formal shared vocabularies and linked data patterns connect datasets across domains.

04 / 04

Reusable

Detailed provenance, clear licence terms and thorough quality documentation maximise the utility of archived sensor records.
