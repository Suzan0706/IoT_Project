Metadata principles / Built on FAIR from the first byte.

F
Findable

Every dataset receives a persistent identifier, rich Dublin Core descriptors, and is indexed by domain, sensor and location.

01 / 04
A
Accessible

Records are retrievable via standard protocols, with explicit licensing and access conditions stated up front.

02 / 04
I
Interoperable

Common vocabularies for sensors, units and frequencies — readable by humans, machines and downstream analytics pipelines.

03 / 04
R
Reusable

Clear provenance, quality scoring and citation guidance — so the next researcher can build on, not re-collect, your work.

04 / 04
